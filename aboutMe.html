ABOUT ME

Hello, I am Lola.
I'm 25 years old and I've been interested
in taking photos since I was a 12-year-old
little pre-teen.

For most part, I've been taking photos of
myself and my closed ones. Once I've had the
chance to travel more, I've also been trying
to capture moments more and more.

Photography has been an on-and-off hobby for
me, but lately it's been feeling more like a
passion than a hobby.

If there is anything you should learn from
photography. I do believe, it's to see beauty
in everything, from traditional to unconventional.
From yourself to other people. From nature to
man-made things.

I enjoy discovering and trying out new DIY
photography ideas and then try to adapt
the new skills to making videos as well.

I've mostly dabbled in making videos
for my own social media, but
I've considered trying to make content
for other people or businesses too.

So far the only obstacle with taking all
of this further is the lack of equipment.
Luckily, that forces one to be creative.

It's important to surround yourself with
people who inspire you and want to see you
do well in whatever you are doing.

A little bit more about me.
I'm an Early Childhood Education teacher
as well as a receptionist at a hotel front office.

I am currently a freshman studying
Business Information Technology at Haaga-Helia
University of Applied Sciences.

I find it important to keep learning
new skills and getting outside of one's comfort
zone. Whether it is an entirely new career or
something simpler, like going to the cinema alone.
